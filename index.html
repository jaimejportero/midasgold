How it works

Without revealing the exact mechanics (that protects both the strategy and the copiers themselves), here's what you can know:

01

Systematic, not discretionary

Every trade follows fixed, tested rules. No emotional decisions and no manual intervention while a trade is developing.

02

Fixed lot size, no martingale

Position size is never increased to "recover" previous losses. Risk per trade is constant and known in advance.

03

Capped exposure

Position accumulation has a fixed ceiling — exposure never grows without limit, no matter how far the market moves.

04

News awareness

The system adjusts its behavior around major economic releases rather than trading blindly through them.

05

Risk management over raw returns

We'd rather sit out a week than force trades in conditions that don't warrant it.

⚠️

Leverage is not optional

1000:1 minimum is the single most important setting before you connect. At lower leverage, the same market move this system is built to withstand can trigger a real margin call before the trade has a chance to play out — this isn't a suggestion, it's the difference between a normal drawdown and a blown account.

Frequently asked questions

What most people ask before they start copying.

What do I need to copy Midas Gold?

An Axi account connected to the Pelican Trading marketplace, where you can subscribe directly to the signal. The sign-up process is the standard one for any copy trading setup on Axi.

What leverage do I need?

1000:1 is the recommended minimum, no exceptions. With lower leverage (500:1 or below), the same market move the system is designed to withstand can trigger a real margin call before the trade plays out as intended.

What's the fee?

25% performance fee — charged only on real profits, never on capital and never as a flat fee.

What was the biggest drawdown?

The toughest event so far was late June / early July 2026, with a sustained gold sell-off that produced significant negative floating P&L over several hours before reversing. The system wasn't manually touched and the trade closed positive. That intraday floating figure was considerably higher than the balance-based drawdown shown in the headline stats -- they're two different metrics, and we think you deserve to know both, not just the one that looks better.

Does the system use a Stop Loss?

Not on individual trades -- risk is managed at the basket level, with dynamic close targets. That's intentional within the system's logic, not an oversight.

What minimum capital do you recommend?

More important than capital itself is leverage (see above). Accounts with very tight capital, even at the recommended minimum leverage, can still come under pressure during extreme volatility events like the one mentioned above. As a rough reference, the live track record above runs on €1,000 starting capital.

What broker and platform do I need?

Axi, via the Pelican Trading copy-trading marketplace. The signal is not distributed through any other broker or platform — be wary of anyone offering "Midas Gold" access elsewhere.

Can I stop copying at any time?

Yes. Copy trading subscriptions on Axi/Pelican can be paused or cancelled at any time from your own account — there's no lock-in period.

Where can I ask questions or follow day-to-day activity?

On the Telegram channel @midasbotgold, where we share updates, results, and market context with the same transparency as here.

Got a question before you start?

If anything above isn't clear, or you want to ask something specific about your situation before connecting your account, reach out directly. We respond on the support channel, kept separate from the general updates channel.
